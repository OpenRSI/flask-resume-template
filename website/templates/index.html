Ecole 42 Paris

Computer Science curriculum• since November 2021

I studied at school 42 because it is a unique institution that offers a singular education model. At school 42, the focus is on project-based learning and problem-solving, rather than traditional classroom instruction and exams.

This allows students to learn practical skills and apply them in real-world situations. In addition to the hands-on learning experience, school 42 also has a strong emphasis on peer-to-peer learning. This means that students are encouraged to collaborate and learn from each other, which helps to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment.

During my time at school 42, I learned a wide range of skills, including programming, data analysis, and critical thinking. I also had the opportunity to work on various projects, including developing a web application and participating in a hackathon. Overall, my experience at school 42 was extremely rewarding and has prepared me well for the challenges of the workforce. I am grateful for the unique education I received and the skills I developed during my time there.

Work

BNP Paribas Fortis CIB

Counterparty and Financing Risk Analyst • 2019 - 2021

I spent two years working as a Counterparty and Financing Risk Analyst on derivatives at BNPP Fortis. In this role, I have been responsible for analyzing and managing the risks associated with the company's financial transactions involving derivatives. This has included evaluating the creditworthiness of the company's counterparties, and determining the appropriate level of financing for these transactions, mainly through collateralization. I have also monitored changes in the financial markets, and provided timely and accurate advice on how to manage the company's risks in this area.
